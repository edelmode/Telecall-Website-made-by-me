FAQs

What courses does Telecall Laguna offer?

Telecall Laguna offers a wide range of computer training courses including web development, data science, networking, cybersecurity, graphic design, and more.


Can I enroll in multiple courses simultaneously?

Yes, you can enroll in multiple courses at Telecall Laguna based on your interests and availability.


What are the class timings at Telecall Laguna?

Telecall Laguna offers flexible class timings including morning, and afternoon, sessions to accommodate students with different schedules.


Does Telecall Laguna offer online courses?

Yes, Telecall Laguna offers both in-person and online courses to cater to the diverse needs of students.


Is financial aid available for students?

Yes, Telecall Laguna provides various financing options and scholarships for eligible students. You can inquire about financial aid during the enrollment process.


Are there any prerequisites for enrolling in courses at Telecall Laguna?

The prerequisites vary depending on the course. Some courses may require basic knowledge of programming or specific software. You can check the course descriptions for more details.
